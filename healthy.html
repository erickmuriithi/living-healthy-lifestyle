HEALTHY LIFESTYLE

DIET.

Your regular diet in day to day life plays a vital role in your body i.e. physical outlook and biological functioning. Junky foods and fast foods should be avoided at all cost they are full of cholesterol and fat which are responsible for heart diseases.Your meal should compose of carbohydrates, protein, vitamins and fruits. You should also never skip a meal.

EXERCISES.

Exercise is a core thing in a healthy lifestyle. Not only does it keep your body fit but also help in proper blood flow in the body. It is essential to spend most of your free time doing physical exercises like bike riding, a walk, stair climbing, yoga etc.

FLUID

Fluids in your body are very essential since it prevents dehydration which leads to headaches ,fatigue and confusion. Fluids also protects and cushion joints and organs. To avoid it make sure you drink a lot of fluid (recommend water atleast eight glass day). If you are a beer lover its best for you to either stop or reduce your intake rate.
